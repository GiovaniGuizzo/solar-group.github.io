Software Optimisation, Learning and Analytics Research (SOLAR) is a group of researchers working on software engineering problems at the interface of software optimisation, machine/evolutionary learning and analytics.
The group is led by Dr Federica Sarro at University College London (UCL) and currently consists of 10 members.
