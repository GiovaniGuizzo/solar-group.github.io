Software Optimisation, Learning and Analytics Research (SOLAR)


SOLAR is a group of researchers working on software engineering problems at the interface of software optimisation, machine/evolutionary learning and analytics.
The group is led by Dr Federica Sarro at University College London (UCL) and currently consists of 10 members.

Members

  • Dr Federica Sarro, Associate Professor at UCL
  • Prof Mark Harman, Part-time Professor at UCL and Research Scientist at Facebook London
  • Dr Giovani Guizzo, Research Fellow at UCL
  • Dr Maria Kechagia, Research Fellow at UCL
  • Dr Alessio Petrozziello , Honorary Senior Researcher at UCL and Senior Data Scientist at Expedia
  • Dr Jie Zhang, Research Fellow at UCL
  • Dario Asprone, PhD candidate at UCL
  • Max Hort, PhD candidate at UCL
  • Rebecca Moussa, PhD candidate at UCL
  • Vali Tawosi, PhD candidate at UCL

Joining SOLAR

We are actively looking for talented students who are interested in joining SOLAR as research fellows, PhD students, Master students and undergraduate research assistants.
Please contact Dr Federica Sarro for more information and include in your message the following: your CV, a statement of your research interests and past experience, starting time, transcripts.
